Headache Logger

Headache Logger is a local-first iPhone and Apple Watch app for one-tap headache tracking. It automatically captures surrounding health, time, and weather context, then lets you export a doctor-friendly CSV whenever you want.

The app does not require an account and does not use advertising or analytics SDKs.
