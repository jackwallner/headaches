Headache Logger Support

Headache Logger is a one-tap iPhone and Apple Watch app for logging headaches with surrounding health and weather context.

Contact

For help, feature requests, or bug reports, email jackwallner@gmail.com.

Troubleshooting

  • If Apple Health context is missing, confirm Health permissions are enabled for Headache Logger in the Health app and iOS Settings.
  • If weather or air quality context is missing, confirm Location access is enabled and that the device has network access when you log the headache.
  • If the Apple Watch app says the log is queued, open the iPhone app and make sure the watch is paired and the companion app is installed.
  • If export fails, try again and verify there is available device storage and a share destination selected.
