- No account or login is required.
  - The main experience is the Headache Logger tab on iPhone and the watch companion app.
  - On first launch, a short onboarding flow explains the app and requests HealthKit and Location
  permissions. Both are optional - if either is denied, the app still logs the headache with partial
  context.
  - The History tab exports a CSV through the standard iOS share sheet.
  - The About tab contains a temperature unit toggle, permission status, and links for privacy policy
  and support.
  - The Apple Watch app queues headache entries to the paired iPhone using WatchConnectivity.
  - The Home Screen widget logs a quick headache event; the main app enriches it with full context on
  next open.
  - The app does not use non-exempt encryption.
