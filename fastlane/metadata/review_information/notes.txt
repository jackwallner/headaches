No account or login is required.

Core flow: Onboarding (optional HealthKit + Location) → Log tab (one-tap headache) → History (CSV export) → Patterns (Pro insights teaser or full insights) → Settings (Pro, restore, legal links).

Pro IAP (same entitlement):
- com.jackwallner.headachelogger.pro.yearly (7-day free trial for eligible new subscribers)
- com.jackwallner.headachelogger.pro.monthly
- com.jackwallner.headachelogger.pro.lifetime (non-consumable)

Reach paywall: Settings → locked Proactive Alerts; Patterns → See Pro plans; or trial-offer sheet → See all plans.

Trial sheet (eligible users): may appear after first log, for users with existing logs, or on Patterns. Start trial purchases yearly; See all plans opens full paywall with all three options. Restore on trial sheet and full paywall.

Full paywall: yearly/monthly/lifetime cards, price + auto-renew disclosure under CTA, Restore, Terms (Apple EULA), Privacy Policy. Dismiss with ×.

Active subscribers: Settings → Manage Subscription (system sheet).

Watch: logs via WatchConnectivity to paired iPhone. Widget enriches on next app open.

No backend; weather from Open-Meteo at log time only. No non-exempt encryption.
