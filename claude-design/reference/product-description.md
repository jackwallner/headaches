## App Store name

**One Tap Headache Tracker**

## Subtitle

Simple headache & migraine log

## Long description

One Tap Headache Tracker helps you capture a headache the moment it starts.

Tap once on iPhone or Apple Watch and the app records the time immediately, then fills in as much surrounding context as it can automatically.

The app can capture:

- Time context — weekday, hour, minute, time zone, and part of day
- Apple Health context — steps, activity, sleep, heart metrics, SpO₂, VO₂ max, breathing, audio exposure, mindful minutes, and recent workouts
- Weather and environment — temperature, barometric pressure, air quality, UV index, and pollen-style signals
- Optional severity rating and personal notes on any event

Other features:

- One-tap Apple Watch logging that syncs back to your paired iPhone
- Home screen widget for logging without opening the app
- Siri Shortcuts support
- CSV export for sharing your history with a doctor

One Tap Headache Tracker is local-first:

- No account required
- No ads
- No analytics SDKs
- No cloud sync
- Manual export only when you decide to share data

## Voice cues

- Direct and warm. Plainspoken. Confident without being clinical.
- No emojis. No "AI-powered." No "revolutionary."
- Verbs over adjectives. "Log a headache" beats "Effortlessly chronicle your migraines."
- Numbers welcome — "50+ data points", "60 columns", "12 headaches logged".
- Never imply diagnosis or treatment. Pattern *observation*, not medical advice.
